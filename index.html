campus life

Library

Libraries support learning within the pedagogic goals of the school. Lancer’s library has a vast collection of books for students to read and develop a passion for reading. The school library is a store house of books, a temple of learning, a place to explore and rejuvenate. The library at Lancers stocks a total of 23,400 books, both in the junior and senior library, housing a wide range of authors. The great classics written by authors like CHARLES DICKENS, THOMAS HARDY, JANE AUSTEN and WILLIAM SHAKESPEARE are all time favourites with the children. Many of the famous Indian writers like KIRAN DESAI, R.K NARAYAN, ANITA DESAI have given birth to many budding writers in the literary firmament of Lancer's. With Harry Potter they get teleported to the mysterious world of 'sorcery and magic'.Lancers hosts many budding poets and authors.The purpose of having a well equipped library at Lancer's is to inculcate critical literacy in Lancerian's and create independently thinking individuals.


English Lab

The school expedites the students’ progress and embraces technology in its entire splendor to benefit the students. Concurring with the fact that technology is now influencing pedagogical choices and with this conviction, Lancer’s provides Language Labs where Language is taught as a tool for communication through a blend of theme, skill and task based learning. Content is available for various levels including Elementary, Beginners, Intermediate and Advanced. Phonics, Vocabulary (general and specific), Reading Comprehension Passages, Listening Passages, thematic topics are some of the areas covered to cater to all four linguistic skills namely Listening, Speaking, Reading and Writing. An early exposure to these shall give students an edge when going for competitive exams like IELTS and TOEFL.






Physics Lab

The Physics Lab is equipped with all the latest scientific instruments required to increase the comprehensive learning, analytical and logical thinking of students .It enables them to use their knowledge in daily life. A variety of Physics apparatus is available to cover the technical fields of electronics, optics , electricity etc. thus helping the students to use their theoretical knowledge to understand the principle, construction and working of various machines used for industrial purposes. IX and X students make frequent visits to the lab so as to get familiar with the physical terminology and phenomena. A lot of emphasis is laid on regular demonstrations and activities for class XI and XII students. They are also guided to do the project work from time to time which helps them to learn-while-doing the activity.



Chemistry Lab

The Chemistry lab is adequately equipped with all the modern apparatus pertaining to the development of research and analytical temperament of the students. A well stocked and furnished cupboard, a separate balance room and a separate store add to the large and spacious laboratory .Special apparatus like desiccators, crucibles and various fractionating columns to support investigatory projects are also available in the laboratory. The practice of the reactions and scientific mechanism is introduced to the students as early as in class IX with various activities and demonstrations during frequent lab visits. For students of Class XI and Class XII, salt analysis, qualitative analysis of organic compounds and volumetric analysis besides a variety of core experiments prepare the students for the industry.



Biology Lab

Biology Lab is a valuable and essential part of any Biology Course. At Lancer's, the Biology lab is well-equipped to organize experiments and other related activities. Lab activities boost various practical skills and reinforce the information gathered in the class. The young scientist study Anatomy and Morphology of organisms in the Biology lab. It is a hub of activities that not only helps to enhance skills but also generates interest in the subject.






Computer Lab

The computer lab caters to the need of today’s generation and keeps them abreast with the latest technology in a well-equipped high-end client-server setup, capable of handling client requests of 100 odd computers simultaneously. There are two labs, one for Juniors and the other one for Seniors . Both the labs can accommodate the complete strength of the class . The lab is equipped with LCD Monitors, Laser printers and speakers for making learning Innovative and Interactive. The computer lab also boasts of a centralized software, through which a teacher can share her screen content with every computer in lab and can give the child a personalized demo experience . Also the lab is frequented by the students and teachers for the research work and preparation of upcoming events.







Robotics

’Robotics’ have revolutionized the concept of learning and brought technology to the class rooms. At Lancer’s we provide a structured course to the students wherein they program their robots to perform various operations. The school has a fully equipped Robotics Lab which allows students to access science and technology via multiple angles. Robotics opens up a plethora of opportunities for the students. Industries across a range of sectors such as automotive and atomic energy use Robotic technologies. We at Lancers, in association with Robotics Wizards have successfully inculcated Educational Robotics in our curriculum. It is a broad term that refers to a collection of activities, instructional programs, physical platforms, educational resources and pedagogical philosophy directed with an objective to provide a set of experiences to facilitate the student's development of knowledge, skills and attitudes for the design, analysis, application and operation of robots. Students are also given opportunity to demonstrate their acquired skills in competitions like Robo-Cups. Robotics is introduced to students from as early as class 4 and continues till class 10. A competent faculty regular sessions and a set of Robo kit arm the students with the required skills.

History

All great ideas begin with a commitment to the future. It is with this steadfast endeavour and in the quest for excellence that Reverend Sqdn. Ldr. A.S.Mann spearheaded the project of establishing the school with the eminent apex body of the Anand Education Society. Thus, the dream child ‘Century Tagore School’ in Ashok Vihar (1974) was born. The holistic education provided by the school soon earned a good name and the need for a spacious school, which could accommodate more students, was felt. With the divine blessings of Bhagwan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, four acres of land was allotted at Prashant Vihar. Thus Lancer’s Convent, Senior Secondary School, one of the premier institutions of North Delhi, affiliated to CBSE came into being. Since then, the school has contributed immensely to nurture intellectual property who are leaving their significant mark in the global arena. The Tree of Knowledge is growing since aeons. Lancer's International is a venture of the Lancer’s Group of Schools. It flaunts a picturesque campus with state – of – art infrastructure. Besides world class - rooms and labs, the school has aesthetically designed sports grounds, tennis court, basket-ball court, residential facilities for students, academic resources, cafeteria and many more facilities to make a satisfying living for the students.
